SHELL PROGRAM

Our “big class” at Shell road registers up to 25 children, 30 months - 5 years old. The class spends half of the outside in the forest or garden, and the remainder of the day focuses on project-based learning and our social-emotional literacy program. Children are not required to be potty trained to attend; meals are provided as part of this program.

INFANTS PROGRAM

Our infant-toddler classroom at Shell Road registers up to 12 children under 36 months old. Registration is by development, requiring children to be on one nap each day, walking, and eating full solids. Meals are provided as part of this program.

LONDON PROGRAM

Our London classroom registers up to 23 children, 3 - 6 years old. This JK/SK program boasts a social-emotional literacy curriculum, and spends the majority of time outside each day. Our outdoor locations include Garry Point Park, London Farm, Britannia Shipyards, Gilbert Beach and Steveston Village.
