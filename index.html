Alderwood Shell

Our “big class” at Shell road registers up to 25 children, 30 months - 5 years old. The class spends half of the outside in the forest or garden, and the remainder of the day focuses on project-based learning and our social-emotional literacy program. Children are not required to be potty trained to attend; meals are provided as part of this program.

Alderwood London

Our London classroom registers up to 23 children, 3 - 6 years old. This JK/SK program boasts a social-emotional literacy curriculum, and spends the majority of time outside each day. Our outdoor locations include Garry Point Park, London Farm, Brittania Shipyards, Gilbert Beach and Steveston Village.

Due to the offsite nature of the program, children must be fully potty-trained and meals are not provided as a part of this program.
